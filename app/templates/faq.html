FAQ

How to post a job on ForeverKenyan

1. Navigate to post jobs > post new job link.

2. You will be directed to sign in or register page (if either your account is logged out or you don't have an account already).

3. Add all the information about your job post such as job title, category, job schedule, county, job responsibilities, Education level, Experience Requirements, Additional requirements and Salary).

4. After filling out the required fields, click on “Post” button.

5. Your job post will be published shortly.

6. Once your job is live, you will receive a notification on the website and an email.

7. Be ready to receive numerous incoming calls and emails from your potential employees.

How to post a product/service on ForeverKenyan

1. Navigate to marketplace > post new product/service link.

2. You will be directed to sign in or register page (if either your account is logged out or you don't have an account already).

3. Add all the information about your post such as title, category, county, Additional details and price).

4. After filling out the required fields, click on “Next” button.

5. On the next page, you will add images for the product/service

6. After uploading all images, click on “Finish” button.

7. Your post will be published shortly.

6. Once your job is live, you will receive a notification on the website and an email.

7. Be ready to receive numerous incoming calls and emails from your potential buyers.

How to find job on ForeverKenyan

1. Click on the email or phone icons on the job post to contact your potential employer
2. Click on the ‘Apply’ button and fill out the form fields which include:

- Your First name
- Your Last name
- Email
- Phone Number
- Message to the recruiter

After you fill the required fields click "Submit" button

A notification and email will be send to the recruiter about you proposal

You can always upload additional documents after the job proposal has been submitted which includes pdfs and more..

Wait for a response from the recruiter.

Posting rules

Following rules are required while you are posting adverts on ForeverKenyan:

Every advert must have a precise title.

Every advert should contain only unique images (taken by the seller and not downloaded from the Internet) without any contact information or watermarks.

Every post must contain images.

Make sure you chose an appropriate category.

The prices of your items must correspond to the real prices of similar products.

All items and products must be legally permitted.

Each item for sale must be posted separately. You cannot post several products within one and the same advert.

How to buy goods on ForeverKenyan

1. Choose the product/service you like

2. Check all the information including Price, a region of sale, Seller's phone number and email to discuss all the details, negotiate about the price, or make your offer. The sellers social media accounts might also be available.

How to unsubscribe from email notifications

To unsubscribe from our email notifications, go to the email send and click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email.

You can Report on illegal/fraudulent activity if you find something suspicious about the seller or his advert. Use the "Report abuse" button. Your feedback will be immediately redirected to our support team and we will block the seller if he proves to be a scammer.
